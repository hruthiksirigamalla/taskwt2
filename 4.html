Jadavpur University is a public state technical and research university located in Jadavpur, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. It was established in 1905 as Bengal Technical Institute and was converted into Jadavpur University in 1955.[4] [5] In 2020, it was ranked fifth among universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework.
